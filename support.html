Frequently Asked Questions

Open DailyVerse and go to Settings → Notifications. Toggle Daily Verse on and choose your preferred delivery time. Make sure notifications are also enabled for DailyVerse in your device's Settings → Notifications menu.

Navigate to Settings → Translation and select from the available translations. Your choice is applied immediately across the app, including the daily verse, search results, and bookmarks.

While viewing any verse, tap the bookmark icon in the top-right corner. You can find all saved verses under the Bookmarks tab. Bookmarks sync across your devices when you are signed in.

Tap the share icon on any verse screen. You can share as plain text, a styled image card, or a direct link. The system share sheet lets you send it via Messages, Mail, social media, or any other app on your device.

Long-press on an empty area of your home screen, tap the + button in the top-left corner, and search for DailyVerse. Choose from small, medium, or large widget sizes, then tap Add Widget. The widget updates automatically with each day's verse.

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, bookmarks, and all associated data from our servers. Alternatively, you can email support@dailyverse-app.com with a deletion request and we will process it within 48 hours.

DailyVerse is free to download and use. Core features — including the daily verse, bookmarks, sharing, and the home screen widget — are available at no cost. A premium subscription unlocks additional translations, themes, and offline access.

The app currently supports the following translations:

  • King James Version (KJV)
  • New International Version (NIV)
  • English Standard Version (ESV)
  • New Living Translation (NLT)
  • New American Standard Bible (NASB)
  • The Message (MSG)

We are continually working to add more translations. If you'd like to request one, please reach out via the contact form below.
