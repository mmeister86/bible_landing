Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Bible App ("we," "our," or "us") handles your data. We are a locally-focused Bible reading application that prioritizes your privacy. We do not collect, share, or sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Favorite Verses: When you save verses to your favorites, the verse reference, text, book name, chapter number, verse number, translation name, and timestamp are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identity information
  • Location data
  • Camera or photo access
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers

2.3 Automatically Stored Local Preferences

The following preferences are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults:

  • Selected Bible translation
  • Appearance mode (light/dark/system)
  • Font size preference
  • Show verse numbers toggle
  • Notification settings (enabled/disabled, reminder time)
  • Recent search history (stored locally, not sent to any server)

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Bible API

The app fetches Bible verse content from bible-api.com. When you search for or request a verse:

  • Data sent: The Bible reference you request (e.g., "John 3:16")
  • Data received: The verse text from the API

We have no control over the data practices of bible-api.com. For their privacy policy, please visit: https://bible-api.com

4. Notifications

If you enable daily reminder notifications:

  • We request permission to send you local notifications
  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device
  • You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or system settings

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All personal data (favorite verses) is stored locally on your device
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We do not transmit your saved verses to any external server

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. All features are appropriate for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the App Store.
